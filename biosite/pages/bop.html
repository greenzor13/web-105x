Bait on Plate

Bait on Plate is a mini comic part of a larger collection of comics taking place in a demon other-world. BoP focus's specifically on skits with Henry Beelzelbub, and often Blair, his roommate. Henry is a famous chef, and well known in the demon culinary industry, and lets us take a look into the lives of the famous faces seen in the main comic.
